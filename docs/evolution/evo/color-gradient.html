Font Style

Font-family styles for HTML tags such as <p> and <h1>.

evo-font-[style]

evo-font-sans-serif

Sans-Serif experience design made simple
for living design systems

evo-font-serif

Serif experience design made simple
for living design systems

evo-font-monospace

Monospace experience design made simple

evo-font-cursive

Cursive experience design made simple
for living design systems

evo-font-fantasy

Cursive experience design made simple
for living design systems

Create an HTML tag suitable for displaying text such as <p> or <h1>, and apply an evo-font-[style] class. The default font family can be adjusted in _variables.scss. 


   <p class="evo-font-sans-serif"></p>
   <p class="evo-font-serif"></p>
   <p class="evo-font-monospace"></p>
   <p class="evo-font-cursive"></p>
   <p class="evo-font-fantasy"></p>

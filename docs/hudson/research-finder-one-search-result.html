Rapid Assessment of Habitat and Wildlife Losses from Hurricane SANDY in the Hudson Raritan Estuary

Colin Grubel, John Waldman, Jim Lodge, and Dennis Suszkowski 2012. Hudson River Foundation, New York, NY

Immediate following Superstorm SANDY, HRF in partnership with Queens College conducted a survey of resource managers, academics, representatives from NGOs, and knowledgeable private citizens to assess the impacts of Hurricane Sandy on fish and other species within and around the Hudson-Raritan Estuary, including tributaries and watersheds of the Hudson and Raritan Rivers. The Report was submitted to the National Fish and Wildlife Foundation on December 12, 2012.

Our Culture

  • Our Chelsea studio is homebase and always available. We encourage working remotely. Work from home. Work from bars, cafe's, libraries, hotels. Work from a beach or a forest. Work at the Client's location.

  • Our hours are roughly 10am to 6pm. But we have a flex-time lifestyle, with humans adapting their schedule to balance thier needs and the project's needs.

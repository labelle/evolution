Text Leading

Space between sentence lines (line-height). Examples with overlapping text are demonstrated because in cases of extreme text size, capitalization, or font-family these options may work best.

text-leading

Try a thing you haven’t done three times. Once, to get over the fear of doing it. Twice, to learn how to do it. And a third time, to figure out whether you like it or not. —Virgil Garnett Thomson

<p class="text-leading">Try a thing you haven’t done three times. Once, to get over the fear of doing it...</p>

Create a container for your text such as a <p> tag or <h1> tag, and apply the text-leading class. Global leading is defined in _variables.scss

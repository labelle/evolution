<h6 class="font-900"></h6>

Use a font-[number] class such as font-900 to define font-weight as a numerical value. Generally, font-500 would be a regular weight, while font-300 would be lighter or thinner, and font-700 heavier or more bold. These classes may not always work, depending on your fonts.

Strategy

Brand Positioning

The brand positioning provides the guiding direction for our organization.

Spark Digital is a Customer Intimate firm. The company’s promise to the market is that we will treat them better than anyone else. The company is driven and organized to fulfill this promise.

Spark Digital produces excellent work and we pride ourselves on quality but we recognize that we are not the only firm capable of doing the kind of work we do.

Spark Digital is price competitive, but do not claim or aim to be the lowest cost solution. Our focus is on value not cost.

Copy

Voice & Tone

The copy voice and tone is short, friendly, and approachable.

Conversational, natural language. We don’t try to sound smart or experienced - we prove that by what we do, not what we say. We are relentlessly transparent.

We speak like we’re talking to a friend... because we are.



Work hard. Stay humble. Have Fun.

You only live once. Better do work you’re proud of and have some fun.



Anchored in the feeling of confidence.

We are selling peace of mind.



We only do what we do well. Our expertise is digital content. We work with medium to large companies in Communications, Media and Technology industries. Given the the proliferation of content being created and managed by companies in every sector from healthcare to finance and higher education, it’s easy to see how our experience might come to serve more industries, but we will not pursue opportunities where our expertise is not a tangible advantage to the client.



Value Proposition

DevSpark brings sanity and humanity to digital product development.
What makes us different is our experience and the way we treat people. We are comfortable and confident doing what we do because we’ve been doing it for years. We treat people like people, not like clients or staff - we make sure that when you work with us you feel confident in the work being done at every step along the way.



About Us

Spark Digital is an experienced group of strategists, designers and developers. We specialize in creating custom software products to distribute, monetize and analyze digital content in all forms. What makes us different is the depth of our experience and the way we treat people. We listen to our clients, take care to fully understand the vision and involve them at every step along the way. In these ways we reduce client risk and take the stress out of building exceptional solutions of enduring value. We measure our success on our clients success.



What Makes Us Different

We’re experienced.
We only take on jobs where our experience will be a tangible advantage to the client. As former CTOs and Products Leads ourselves we understand our clients challenges and needs.
We put the client first.
We solicit feedback and accept criticism openly. By listening to what our clients have to say we improve the way we work.
We’re flexible.
We tailor our services to what the client needs. We will never try to tailor the customer need to our services.
We’re focused.
We only do what we do well. We realize we can’t do everything, and we’re cool with that.
We’re humble.
We have never and will never rest on our laurels. We know, that in our industry, once you think you know it all, you begin to fail.
We’re full service.
We are strategists, designers and developers...
We’re always learning.
We teach each other something new everyday. We invest in training and betterment of our people.



Our Core Values

Put Clients First

Own Results

Solve Problems

Be Agile

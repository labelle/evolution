Image Ratio

The .image-ratio-[size] classes are helpful for displaying containers with background images displayed at a fixed ratio. .image-ratio-1-1 is a square. .image-ratio-16-9 is a popular video ratio of 16:9. .image-ratio-9-16 is 16:9 but vertically oriented.
